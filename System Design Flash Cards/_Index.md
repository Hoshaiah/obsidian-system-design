# Flashcards — System Design (Pareto Decks)

The high-yield 20% of system design that covers ~80% of interview moments. Each note below is its own **subdeck** (tag `flashcards/<topic>`) for the Obsidian *Spaced Repetition* plugin — review them all together, or drill into one.

## Decks
- [[Framework & Delivery]] — the spine you run on every problem
- [[Estimation]] — back-of-envelope numbers to have automatic
- [[Databases & Storage]] — picking the store and justifying it
- [[Caching & Performance]] — read-path speed + the hazards
- [[Messaging & Streams]] — queues, logs, ordering, delivery
- [[Scaling & Distribution]] — replication, sharding, hashing
- [[Consistency & Theory]] — CAP/PACELC, consistency models
- [[Dilemmas]] ⭐ — the "X vs Y, when each?" calls that win interviews

## How to use
- **Pareto rule:** these are deliberately the *most reused* ideas. Master these cold before adding breadth.
- The `Dilemmas` deck is the differentiator — interviewers probe tradeoffs, and a crisp "X when…, Y when…, and I'd switch if…" reads as senior.
- Add your own cards from *your* misses — those are worth 10× pre-made ones.
- All cards use `Question::Answer` (single-line) or a lone `?` line (multi-line). Review progress saves as `<!--SR:-->` comments inside each file.
